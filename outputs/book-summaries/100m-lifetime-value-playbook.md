## Tactical Ideas
### Upsell with more of, or more help with, what they just bought
- **ID:** `100m-lifetime-value-playbook-005`
- **Problem(s) Solved:**
  - Helps businesses design premium upsells that feel obvious and low-friction to the buyer.
- **How It Works:** Hormozi's favorite upsell pattern is more of the same outcome or more assistance getting it, but faster, with less risk, effort, or hassle. This keeps the upsell anchored to why the customer bought in the first place.
- **Usage Example(s):**
  - A course creator can upsell private implementation help or faster feedback loops instead of introducing a brand-new unrelated topic.
- **Source Notes:** Sections: Start story; The Crazy Eight
- **Related Items:** None

### Use money math to set the baseline before optimizing
- **ID:** `100m-lifetime-value-playbook-006`
- **Problem(s) Solved:**
  - Helps owners avoid guessing which monetization lever matters most.
- **How It Works:** Before changing the model, the book pushes the reader to calculate current gross profit, repeat rate, and customer economics. The right lever is much easier to spot once the baseline is visible.
- **Usage Example(s):**
  - A subscription business can calculate gross profit per member, average tenure, and add-on take rate before deciding whether the fastest gain is pricing, add-ons, or retention.

## Strategic Ideas
### Higher LTV lets you win the ad auction
- **ID:** `100m-lifetime-value-playbook-002`
- **Problem(s) Solved:**
  - Helps teams see monetization strategy as a growth lever rather than a backend afterthought.
- **How It Works:** Hormozi argues that the business that can make a customer worth more can outspend competitors on acquisition. LTV is therefore what lets an advertising engine scale without breaking.
- **Usage Example(s):**
  - An ecommerce brand with stronger repeat purchase and upsell economics can afford to bid more aggressively on Meta than a competitor with a one-and-done product.
- **Source Notes:** Sections: Why Is It So Important?
- **Related Items:** None

### Sell customers the next problem that naturally follows the first purchase
- **ID:** `100m-lifetime-value-playbook-003`
- **Problem(s) Solved:**
  - Helps businesses create backend offers that customers actually want instead of founder pet projects.
- **How It Works:** The opening story shows that the winning upsell was closer to the core product and solved the buyer's next logical need. The strongest backend offers feel like a natural progression, not a random new business line.
- **Usage Example(s):**
  - A company selling basic investing education might upsell advanced implementation, advisory help, or done-with-you planning rather than a totally unrelated offer.

## Frameworks
### LTV is gross profit, not vanity revenue
- **ID:** `100m-lifetime-value-playbook-001`
- **Problem(s) Solved:**
  - Helps businesses make smarter acquisition decisions by measuring what a customer is actually worth after fulfillment costs.
- **How It Works:** The playbook frames LTV as lifetime gross profit, not just topline sales. This matters because a customer who buys a lot but costs nearly as much to fulfill may still be economically weak.
- **Usage Example(s):**
  - An agency charging $3,000 per month can compute what is left after labor and delivery costs before deciding how aggressively it can buy leads.
- **Source Notes:** Sections: What Is LTV?; How To Calculate LTV
- **Related Items:** None

### The Crazy Eight monetization levers
- **ID:** `100m-lifetime-value-playbook-004`
- **Problem(s) Solved:**
  - Helps operators systematically search for more customer value instead of defaulting to just finding more leads.
- **How It Works:** The playbook organizes LTV expansion into eight levers: raise prices, reduce costs, increase purchase frequency, cross-sell, sell more quantity, sell better quality, and use downsells that retain revenue when buyers resist the main offer.
- **Usage Example(s):**
  - A skincare brand could combine a modest price increase, refill reminders, complementary product cross-sells, and a lower-commitment starter bundle to improve LTV from several angles at once.
- **Source Notes:** Sections: The Crazy Eight
- **Related Items:** None
