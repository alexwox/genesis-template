## Strategic Ideas
### Call discipline is part of sales skill
- **ID:** `aqc-closer-handbook-005`
- **Problem(s) Solved:**
  - Helps teams improve conversion through basic operating behavior, not just objection lines.
- **How It Works:** Smiling before dialing, removing distractions, taking notes, and staying fully present sound simple, but they improve first impressions and call quality. Good sales handbooks usually make these behaviors explicit because most reps skip them.
- **Usage Example(s):**
  - A closer team can require reps to have notes open, camera ready, and no multitasking during calls to improve consistency.

### Use structured discovery before pitching
- **ID:** `aqc-closer-handbook-002`
- **Problem(s) Solved:**
  - Helps closers avoid pitching the wrong solution to the wrong pain or level of urgency.
- **How It Works:** A proper closer handbook typically forces the rep to understand current state, desired outcome, timeline, and pain before recommending anything. This keeps the sale anchored in the prospect's words.
- **Usage Example(s):**
  - A rep speaking to an agency prospect can clarify revenue goal, current acquisition issue, and timing before presenting the service offer.
